# The Mirror Protocol: Lake Shadow 🔍🪞

A psychological suspense thriller and puzzle-escape adventure built in **Godot 4**.

## 📖 The Story
You play as **Julian**, a young man haunted by the disappearance of his girlfriend, **Elena**, at Lake Shadow. As you investigate the mystery, you begin to realize that the "ghost" framing you for her disappearance might be closer than you think—literally living within the walls of your own home.

### The Mystery of the Two Episodes:
* **Episode 1: The Vanishing at Lake Shadow** – A high-stakes investigation into Elena's disappearance and the "ghost" stalking the family manor.
* **Episode 2: The Mirror Protocol** – The revelation of **Thomas**, the "fading twin" hidden in a pressurized suite inside the house for 15 years.

---

## 🛠 Project Structure
This project follows a **10-Stage per Episode** structure. Each stage is a unique environment containing 10-15 clues.

### Episode 1 Stages:
1.  **The Evidence Room** (Current Focus) - Break in to retrieve the case file.
2.  **The Apartment** - Elena's preserved living space.
3.  **The Parents’ Attic** - Uncovering blacked-out medical records.
4.  **The Best Friend’s Garage** - Confronting Leo.
5.  **The Lake House Pier** - The first physical encounter with the "Ghost."
6.  **The Boathouse** - Searching for the hidden camera.
7.  **The Garden Maze** - Digging for the missing phone.
8.  **The Doctor’s Office** - The secret tape recording.
9.  **The Old Well** - Finding the memory card.
10. **The Darkroom (Climax)** - Developing the truth.
